What kind of game is this?

  • Low gatekeeping. High consequence.
  • Rules as few as needed, each as impactful as possible.
  • Characters defined by choices, not rigid classes.
  • Worldbuilding support for GMs who want a living sandbox.
  • Expandable enough for politics, warfare, crafting, travel, horror, mystery, and myth.
