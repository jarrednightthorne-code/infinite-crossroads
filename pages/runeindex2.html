Bás
Death — The Necromancer's Rune

“Death is not the opposite of life, but an innate part of it.”

Description

Bás allows a Manipulator to harness death and unlife. A Bás Sigil may target one person, object, or corpse per Mana spent.

Puppets require active Concentration, even if another power source sustains the Sigil. Animations persist until destroyed, so long as they can find Mana sources to drain.

Mana Effects

Drain — 1 ManaSap vitality and reduce physical ability. Pair with a Discipline Rune to define what is drained.
Bolster — 2 ManaIncrease vitality and enhance physical ability. Pair with a Discipline Rune to define what is bolstered.
Puppet — 3 ManaWrest control of an existing corpse as a marionette while you Concentrate.
Siphon — 4 ManaDrain another creature and add that vitality to yourself. Pair with a Discipline Rune to define what is taken.
Animation — 5 ManaInstill a singular purpose into remains. Animated corpses act without direct control.

Material Components

DriftwoodA funerary material tied to endings. Used in hexes, drums, or rattles to hinder enemies or call/control the undead.
JetBlack organic gemstone from Aos'si burial grounds. Associated with nightmares, dread, and necromancy; can substitute for Bás.
Fel-FleshUndead husk hide treated with Yarrow oil and elemental earth. Grants Necrotic Resistance; cursed armor causes Dark Hunger.
DarksteelHaunted black wood immune to rust and fire but rots in bright light. Weapons deal Frost or Necrotic damage; armor resists either.

Spatial Components

CryptsClose to the Violet Tide; effective wells of deathly energy.
CemeteriesBest when unmarked, abandoned, or unsanctified. Sanctified ground is a poor conduit unless the soul is willing and aware.
Killing FieldsMass graves and battle memorials. Powerful sites. Extreme caution advised.

Temporal Components

PloutonThe greater malefic: time, aging, decay, and finality. Highest on the 25th day of Eldermoon.
Feast of the Moon25th of Ivy Moon, when Materia and the Dead Realm draw close.
The DragonThe 12th year of the Templar cycle, treated with caution for its bond with death and Drakōn fear.
